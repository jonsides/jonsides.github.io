my main interests include clinical neuroscience and cognitive science. i am a research member in Prof. Abhilasha Kumar's (bowdoin college) Lexicon Lab (https://thelexiconlab.github.io/).
following experiments by Horchak & Garrido (2021), my current research involves studying the effect of unstated and implied nonvisual information on the mental representation of object
state change in situation models. other lab projects investigate the interaction of phonological and semantic information in memory search.

i am also an aspiring clinical neuroscience researcher, interested in investigating the physiological underpinnings of psychiatric disorders like Major Depressive Disorder, Bipolar Disorder, and ADHD.

additionally, i make really good coffee; i love ice-skating, watching lots of star wars, hockey, and forecasting elections.
